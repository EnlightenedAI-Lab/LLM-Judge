# Contributing to LLM Judge

Thank you for your interest in contributing to LLM Judge.

This project is in active research and early development within the Enlightened AI Research Lab.
At this stage, contributions are limited to collaborators who have been explicitly approved by the lab.

If you have questions, proposals, or wish to collaborate, please contact:

**research@enlightenedaI.ai**

We appreciate your interest in advancing reflective alignment and coherence evaluation.
